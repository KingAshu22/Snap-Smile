Shipping Policy

Order Processing

Once your order is placed, it will be processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with your order details.

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Delivery times are estimates and commence from the date of shipping, rather than the date of order. Delivery times are to be used as a guide only and are subject to the acceptance and approval of your order.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the shipping method chosen and will be added to your order total during checkout. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, which will be noted on our website.

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that international shipments may be subject to import taxes, customs duties, and fees levied by the destination country. The recipient of an international shipment may be subject to such import taxes, customs duties, and fees, which are levied once a shipment reaches the recipient's country.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order through our carrier's website.

Custom-Made Veneers

Our Snap Smile veneers are custom-made specifically for your teeth. Due to the personalized nature of these products, they are non-refundable and cannot be returned or exchanged. We ensure the highest quality in the creation of your veneers to guarantee your satisfaction.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged in transit, please contact us at support@snapsmile.in so we can assist you with a replacement. Please provide your order number and any relevant details in your email.
